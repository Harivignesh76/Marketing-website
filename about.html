About AutoGlow

Discover our story, our team, and our commitment to excellence in car detailing.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, AutoGlow began as a small garage with a big vision: to provide the highest quality car detailing services with unmatched attention to detail. Our founder, Michael Reynolds, a car enthusiast with over 15 years of experience in the automotive industry, started the business with just two employees and a passion for making vehicles look their absolute best.

Over the years, we've grown from that small garage to a full-service detailing center, expanding our services to include professional painting, custom stickering, performance modifications, premium sound systems, PPF coating, and comprehensive interior detailing. What hasn't changed is our commitment to quality, precision, and customer satisfaction.

Today, AutoGlow is recognized as one of the leading car detailing garages in the region, serving thousands of satisfied customers and transforming countless vehicles from ordinary to extraordinary. Our team has grown to include specialists in every aspect of car detailing, each bringing their unique expertise and passion to the work we do.
