Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and booking process.

We recommend booking your appointment at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially for comprehensive services like painting or modifications. For basic services, we may be able to accommodate appointments with shorter notice, but availability varies based on our schedule.

Service times vary depending on the specific service and your vehicle's condition. Basic detailing services may take 2-4 hours, while more comprehensive services like full paint jobs or major modifications can take several days to complete. We'll provide you with a time estimate when you book your appointment.

Yes, we offer warranties on most of our services. The warranty period varies depending on the service type. Paint jobs typically come with a 2-year warranty against defects, PPF coatings with a 5-year warranty, and sound system installations with a 1-year warranty. Please ask about specific warranty terms for your desired service.

For most services, we recommend removing personal belongings from your vehicle before your appointment. For painting or exterior services, a basic wash is helpful but not required. For interior detailing, removing large items and trash will help us provide more thorough service. Our team will provide specific preparation instructions when you book.

Yes, we offer mobile detailing services for basic cleaning and detailing within a 25-mile radius of our location. However, more complex services like painting, modifications, and sound system installations must be performed at our facility where we have the necessary equipment and controlled environment.
